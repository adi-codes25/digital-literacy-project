Social Media Do’s
1. Maintain a professional profile
    Use clear bio, proper username, and decent content.
2. Think before posting
    Ask: “Will this affect my future (job/internship)?”
3. Use privacy settings wisely
    Control who can see your posts and personal info.
4. Verify information before sharing
    Avoid spreading fake news or misinformation.
5. Respect others online
    Be polite and avoid offensive or harmful comments.


Social Media Don’ts:
1. Don’t share personal information publicly
    (Phone number, address, passwords, etc.)
2. Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content
    It can harm your reputation.
3. Don’t engage in cyberbullying or arguments
    Avoid toxic discussions.
4. Don’t click on suspicious links or ads
    They may be scams or phishing attempts.
5. Don’t overshare your daily life
    It can risk your privacy and security.
